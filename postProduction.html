“The sound and music are 50% of the entertainment in a movie” - George Lucas


I would argue that both sound and music is a few more percentage points higher than what the prolific George Lucas said, but I have enough self-awareness to know that I’m a little biased. And until we are able to incorporate one of the other 5 senses (excluding vision) into the large-scale multimedia experience, we must rely on both to deliver and celebrate human expressionism. And while I may not have the keenest eyes for the visual aspect, I have extensive experience using the tools and practices of producing and designing sounds for all multimedia productions overall.
